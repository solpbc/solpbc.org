§4 · 2:20 · what i built

solstone is a personal agent that runs entirely on your device.

sol isn't here to act for you, it's here to know you.

No cloud. No accounts. No authentication. No access to change anything in your world.

You just install it, and it starts experiencing your life with you.

It's fully open source. Everything you do, see, or say stays in your world. Always.
