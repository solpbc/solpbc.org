Everything I've built shares one central theme: trust.

Safety.

Sovereignty.

Respect.

Individuality.

§4 · 2:15 · what i built

solstone is a platform.

solstone hosts a personal agent named sol that runs entirely on your devices.

sol isn't here to do things for you, it's here to know you.

And it builds on these pillars of trust.

Safety: it is fully open source. Everything you share with sol stays in your world. Always.

Sovereignty: no cloud. No accounts. No authentication. No access to change anything in your world.

§5 · 2:50 · respect — onboarding-by-living

Respect: onboarding solstone is different.

You give it permission to share in your experiences.

And then nothing. For days.

It just sits there in the background — collecting and journaling what it sees — building a memory of you and your routines in your daily life.

§5.5 · 3:15 · individuality — sol shows up
