§4 · 2:20 · what i built

solstone is a personal agent that runs entirely on your device.

No cloud. No accounts. No authentication. No access to change anything in your world.

You just install it, and it starts experiencing your life with you.

It's fully open source. Everything you do, see, or say stays on your machine. Always.

§5 · 2:45 · onboarding solstone is different

Onboarding solstone is different.

You give it permission. And then nothing. For days.

It's sitting there, collecting and journaling what it sees — building a memory of your routines and your daily life.

It's not journaling because you told it to.

It's not summarizing because you asked.

It's just observing. Saving and organizing everything.
