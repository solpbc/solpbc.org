sol pbc

the specific public benefit purpose of the corporation is to advance digital self-determination by providing open-source, privacy-respecting tools and services that enable individuals to directly collect and gain insight from their personal data.

founded and solely operated by jeremie miller

governance commitments

founder stewardship

during jer's stewardship as a director, the company's covenants can't be amended without his personal, written consent — a right that cannot be delegated.

continuity beyond the founder

if jer ceases to serve as a director, the resulting board vacancy is filled by a successor designator named in the articles of incorporation (initially our outside counsel, ramon rhymes), who selects a director committed to the same benefit purpose and the same binding covenants.

mission-aligned successors only

any merger, acquisition, or transfer of control is conditional: the successor must be legally bound to preserve a substantially equivalent benefit purpose and must assume covenants no less protective than those in article 8. acquisition by an entity that wouldn't honor these protections is not permitted.

your data is never sold

sol pbc will never sell, license, sublicense, or lease user data — including anonymized, aggregated, or de-identified data — for any form of compensation. period.

strengthening only

after jer ceases to serve as a director, the covenants can be amended only to strengthen these protections or to comply with mandatory law to the minimum extent strictly required. weakening amendments are foreclosed forever.

these commitments are legally binding, filed with the colorado secretary of state.
