what we're building a reusable, brand-safe deck system for deep-tech extro talks — separate from the sol pbc / solstone pitch decks. fixed 16:9, scales to any screen, pure web, animated where it earns it.
decision 01 — canvas

16:9, fixed canvas, scales to the room

yes — modern projectors & big-screen TVs are 16:9 (1920×1080). we design once on a fixed 1280×720 canvas and scale it as one unit to fill any screen. pixel-locked layout, identical everywhere. letterbox bars are just more black — on theme.

why fixed
  • looks identical on jer's laptop & the venue screen
  • no reflow surprises mid-talk
  • box-drawing art stays aligned at any size
  • export any slide to a clean PNG/PDF
