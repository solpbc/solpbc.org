The specific public benefit purpose of the Corporation is to advance digital self-determination by providing open-source, privacy-respecting tools and services that enable individuals to directly collect and gain insight from their personal data.

governance commitments

solo stewardship

sol pbc has exactly one director, one officer, and one employee: Jeremie Miller. No one else can hold these roles during his lifetime.

agentic future

If Jer can no longer serve, leadership transitions to an agentic leader, not a human successor. Jer is developing this agentic leader to operate under the same binding covenants he follows.

no change of control

sol pbc can't be sold, merged, acquired, or reorganized.

your data is never sold

sol pbc will never sell, license, or share owner personal data for any form of compensation. Period.

immutable governance

These commitments can't be changed without Jer's personal, written consent. That consent can't be delegated. After his organic stewardship, the only path is his digital stewardship.

These commitments are legally binding, filed with the Colorado Secretary of State.
